Namaste is a Sanskrit word which translates to “The Divine In Me Bows To The Divine In You.”

Our Mission

The Namaste Foundation is an educational institution where the fundamental objective is to teach the principles of meditation, mindfulness and yoga, to be able to live a present and fulfilling life.
Namaste Foundation is a non-profit entity. All the people you meet at our headquarters, attending the reception, giving the Philosophy, Yoga and Meditation classes, are volunteers.
We believe that there can be a better world and we put our actions at the service of that ideal.
